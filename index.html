About GAN CUBE

GANCUBE Technologies
GAN Elastic System

The GAN Elastic System (GES) uses two repulsion magnetic rings replacing traditional springs, reducing friction and noise, delivering a smoother and quite turning experience. Set a new standard in speed cube technology

GAN Magnetic levitation technology

GAN's Magnetic Levitation technology uses magnetic forces to eliminate friction. This innovation ensures a nearly frictionless, smoother, and faster turning experience, delivering unmatched control and precision.

GAN Magnetic Core

GAN's Magnetic Core Technologhy enhances traditional core designs by aligning magnetic particles with the magnets in the corner pieces
